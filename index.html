Evogen is a dynamic two-day inter-collegiate IT fest hosted by Gogte BCA for the past ten years. This prestigious event serves as a vibrant platform for students to showcase, refine, and elevate their technical prowess through an array of exhilarating activities, including problem analysis, quizzes, software development, and innovative IT-related games.

Designed with a vision to foster personality development, ignite entrepreneurial spirit, and enhance technical expertise, Evogen is more than just a competition—it’s a celebration of innovation, collaboration, and learning. With each passing edition, the challenges grow more thrilling, the interactions more inspiring, and the ideas more ingenious, making Evogen an unmissable festival of knowledge, creativity, and fun!
